    a: "Not always. A $95 fee card that returns $300 in rewards on your actual spending is better than a no-fee card returning $200. Beyond that, no-fee cards are usually the smarter add-ons."

The no-fee nature of these cards makes them especially attractive for balance transfers since you are not paying a fee to access the promotional rate."
